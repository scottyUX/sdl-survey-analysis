Key Findings

  • Mean AU is highest in design; implementation is very close.
  • Mean AUM peaks in implementation.
  • Overall AU and AUM correlate at r = 0.736.
  • Deployment and maintenance show the lowest mean AU and AUM.
  • Within-stage AU–AUM correlation is weakest in implementation and among the strongest in testing.

Interpretive Summary

Students do not use AI uniformly across the SDLC. Usage and maturity track each other overall, but stage-level correlations show that how much students use AI at a stage and how mature that use is can diverge—especially where within-stage AU–AUM correlation is lower.
